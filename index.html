LimaLinks

Web and USSD

LimaLinks is a for-profit company that provides technology solutions to a variety of market actors in the Zambian agriculture sector. Lima Links focus is on building ICT tools to drive agri-business growth. Their Agriculture Platform provides farmers with access to live market prices, a range of agricultural products, suppliers and retail prices, and connects farmers to buyers..

I was responsible for building their Python backend which was used for the first version of the app. It handled about 50, 000 farmers that were registered on the platform at the time I left the team in October 2017. I used the Django framework to create REST APIs that were accessed by the Android, Web and USSD LimaLinks applications.

KiNVOLVED - USA

KiNVOLVED Moodle Site

Designed and setup the KiNVOLVED Moodle site that hosts their user training contents. Using the Moodle OAuth2 features, a Single Sign-on functionality that allows users to access the Moodle instance directly from the KiNVOLVED App without having to sign-in again was setup.

Lessons learnt:

  • Manually coding a by-pass for the confirmation email triggered by the OAuth2 plugin when a user access the Moodle instance from KiNVOLVED App
  • Changing to Moodle font family to a custom one
